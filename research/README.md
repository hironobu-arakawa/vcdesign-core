# Research & External Evaluation

This directory contains third-party research, analytical reviews,
and independent evaluations related to VCDesign.

These materials are **not usage guides** and **not normative specifications**.
They represent external interpretations, validations, and theoretical positioning
of VCDesign within broader software engineering and AI governance contexts.

VCDesign itself remains defined by:
- constitution/
- bindings/
- official docs/

Research documents may reference, reinterpret, or extend these ideas,
but do not redefine them.
